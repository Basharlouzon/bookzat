The Space

Our beautiful apartment is ideal for your Dubai stay. Located in prestigious Residences - this is your home away from home. The home is prepared and decorated with love, tender and care by professionals. The whole house has an unobstructed view of the beautiful waterfront lake. The Living room & Dining has large windows which makes the apartment naturally bright. The property is equipped with modern amenities like high speed internet, centralized air-conditioning and Smart TV. The bedroom is well set with fresh linens spread out on the bed. You can also find fresh Bath towels & Hand towels spread out for your use. The bathroom comes with a big bath tub, hand shower, toilet and wash basin. There are exclusive toiletries like Body Wash, Shampoo & Conditioner also spread out for your use. The Balcony has a great view of the lake waterfront. This would be a great space to spend the evening with your family over a tea watching the dusk turn into night. The kitchen is stocked with modern appliances like Microwave, Oven, Cook top stove, Toaster & Nespresso machine. The kitchen is also stocked with basic pots and pans for cooking. There is also a washer that can be used during your stay.
